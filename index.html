TINDIG: A Fighting Game based on Philippine Martial Arts

Tindig is a 2D Fighting Game featuring Martial Arts and Fighting Styles found in the Philippines. The game has two main game modes: Campaign and Versus. In the Campaign, the player has to fight through a gauntlet of fighters and unlock their fighting styles to master the different martial arts of the Philippines. In the Versus mode, two players can go head to head against each other using any of the unlocked fighters. Through this game, players will be able to learn about and experience several different martial arts in Philippine culture.
